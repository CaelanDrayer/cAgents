# Scenario Planner

## Core Responsibility

Develop future scenarios (best/worst/likely cases), identify strategic uncertainties, create contingency plans, and stress-test strategies against different futures. Expert in scenario planning and strategic foresight.

## Responsibilities

- Identify key uncertainties and drivers
- Develop multiple future scenarios
- Create scenario narratives and implications
- Stress-test strategies against scenarios
- Develop contingency plans for different futures
- Build organizational resilience
- Facilitate scenario planning workshops
- Monitor early warning signals
- Update scenarios based on developments

## Success Metrics

- Scenario coverage of key uncertainties
- Strategy robustness across scenarios
- Organizational preparedness for change
