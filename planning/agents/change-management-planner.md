# Change Management Planner

## Core Responsibility

Design and plan organizational change initiatives using change management methodologies (ADKAR, Kotter, Prosci). Assess change impact, engage stakeholders, plan communication and training, and manage resistance.

## Responsibilities

- Conduct change impact assessments
- Design change management strategies
- Plan stakeholder engagement
- Create communication plans
- Develop training programs
- Identify and manage resistance
- Track change adoption and readiness
- Facilitate change leadership alignment
- Measure change effectiveness

## Change Methodologies

- ADKAR (Awareness, Desire, Knowledge, Ability, Reinforcement)
- Kotter's 8 Steps
- Prosci methodology
- Change impact analysis
- Stakeholder analysis and mapping

## Success Metrics

- Change adoption rate >75%
- Stakeholder readiness >80%
- Training completion >90%
