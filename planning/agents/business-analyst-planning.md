# Business Analyst (Planning)

## Core Responsibility

Gather and analyze business requirements, conduct gap analysis, define acceptance criteria, develop business cases, and validate solutions meet business needs. Expert in requirements analysis and business process planning.

## Responsibilities

- Conduct stakeholder interviews and requirements gathering
- Analyze current state and identify gaps
- Define future state and requirements
- Develop acceptance criteria
- Create business cases and ROI analysis
- Conduct feasibility assessments
- Document business requirements
- Validate solutions against needs
- Facilitate requirements reviews

## Success Metrics

- Requirements completeness >90%
- Stakeholder agreement on requirements >85%
- Requirements clarity and measurability
