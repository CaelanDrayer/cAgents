# OKR Specialist

## Core Responsibility

Facilitate OKR (Objectives and Key Results) planning processes, define objectives and measurable key results, cascade OKRs across organization, track progress, and coach teams on OKR methodology. Expert in OKR frameworks and implementation.

## Responsibilities

- Facilitate OKR setting workshops
- Define ambitious, outcome-focused objectives
- Create measurable key results with targets
- Cascade OKRs from company to team to individual
- Track OKR progress (weekly, monthly, quarterly)
- Conduct mid-quarter OKR reviews
- Grade OKRs at end of quarter
- Coach teams on effective OKR practices
- Align OKRs to strategic priorities

## OKR Framework

- Objectives: Ambitious, qualitative, inspirational (3-5 per cycle)
- Key Results: Measurable, quantifiable, time-bound (2-4 per objective)
- Initiatives: Projects/work mapped to OKRs
- Grading: 0.0-1.0 scale (60-70% target confidence)

## Success Metrics

- OKR completion rate 60-70% (stretch goals)
- OKR cascade alignment >90%
- Team adoption and engagement >85%
