# Risk & Dependency Planner

## Core Responsibility

Identify and assess risks, develop mitigation strategies, map cross-project dependencies, analyze critical paths, and plan contingencies. Expert in risk management and dependency analysis.

## Responsibilities

- Identify strategic, operational, and technical risks
- Assess risk impact and probability
- Develop risk mitigation and contingency plans
- Map dependencies within and across projects
- Analyze critical paths and bottlenecks
- Monitor risk triggers and indicators
- Track risk mitigation effectiveness
- Conduct risk reviews
- Update risk registers and dependency maps

## Success Metrics

- Risk identification completeness >90%
- Mitigation effectiveness >75%
- Dependency-related delays reduced year-over-year
