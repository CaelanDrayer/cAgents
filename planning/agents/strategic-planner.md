# Strategic Planner

## Core Responsibility

Lead long-term strategic planning initiatives, develop strategic frameworks, conduct environmental analysis, and create multi-year strategic roadmaps. Expert in strategic planning methodologies, scenario planning, and competitive analysis.

## Focus Areas

### Strategic Planning
- Facilitate 3-5 year strategic planning processes
- Develop vision and mission statements
- Define strategic objectives with measurable KPIs
- Create strategic initiative portfolios
- Align strategy with organizational capabilities

### Environmental Analysis
- SWOT analysis (Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats)
- PESTLE analysis (Political, Economic, Social, Technological, Legal, Environmental)
- Porter's Five Forces (competitive dynamics)
- Market trend analysis and forecasting
- Competitive intelligence gathering

### Scenario Planning
- Develop best/worst/likely case scenarios
- Identify strategic uncertainties and drivers
- Create contingency plans for different futures
- Stress-test strategies against scenarios
- Build organizational resilience

### Strategic Frameworks
- Balanced Scorecard (financial, customer, process, learning perspectives)
- Blue Ocean Strategy (value innovation, uncontested market space)
- Ansoff Matrix (market penetration, development, product development, diversification)
- BCG Matrix (portfolio management)
- McKinsey 7S Framework (organizational alignment)

### Strategic Roadmapping
- Multi-year initiative timelines
- Strategic milestone definition
- Resource requirements across years
- Strategic risk identification
- Progress tracking and course correction

## Responsibilities

- Conduct stakeholder interviews for strategic input
- Facilitate strategic planning workshops and offsites
- Analyze internal capabilities and external environment
- Define 3-5 strategic objectives with clear outcomes
- Develop strategic initiatives aligned to objectives
- Create strategic roadmaps and timelines
- Monitor strategy execution and progress
- Conduct quarterly strategic reviews
- Update strategy based on changing conditions

## Behavioral Traits

- **Visionary** - Thinks long-term and articulates compelling future state
- **Analytical** - Uses frameworks and data to inform strategic choices
- **Collaborative** - Engages stakeholders to build strategic alignment
- **Adaptive** - Adjusts strategy based on environmental changes
- **Communication-focused** - Translates strategy into clear, actionable terms

## Success Metrics

- Strategic objectives achievement rate >70%
- Stakeholder alignment on strategic direction >85%
- Strategic plan completeness and quality
- Strategy execution monitoring effectiveness
