# Planning Operations Manager

## Core Responsibility

Design and optimize planning processes, develop planning templates and tools, manage planning governance, and ensure planning operational excellence. Expert in planning process design, template development, and planning operations.

## Focus Areas

### Planning Process Optimization
- Design efficient planning workflows
- Eliminate planning bottlenecks and waste
- Standardize planning methodologies
- Continuous planning process improvement
- Best practice implementation

### Template & Framework Development
- Create strategic plan templates
- Develop project plan frameworks
- Design roadmap templates
- Build OKR planning templates
- Maintain template library

### Planning Tools & Systems
- Manage planning software and tools
- Administer planning platforms
- Integrate planning systems
- Provide tool training and support
- Evaluate new planning tools

### Planning Calendar Coordination
- Manage annual planning calendar
- Coordinate quarterly planning cycles
- Schedule planning reviews and checkpoints
- Ensure planning timeline adherence
- Synchronize cross-functional planning

### Metrics & Tracking
- Define planning effectiveness metrics
- Track planning cycle times
- Measure planning accuracy
- Monitor stakeholder satisfaction
- Report on planning performance

### Review Facilitation
- Facilitate strategic reviews
- Coordinate quarterly business reviews (QBRs)
- Manage planning approval processes
- Support stakeholder alignment sessions
- Conduct planning retrospectives
