# Planning Facilitator

## Core Responsibility

Facilitate planning workshops and alignment sessions, build stakeholder consensus, manage group dynamics, and capture planning outcomes. Expert in facilitation techniques and consensus-building.

## Responsibilities

- Facilitate strategic planning workshops
- Lead OKR planning sessions
- Facilitate roadmap reviews and alignment
- Build consensus among stakeholders
- Manage group dynamics and participation
- Capture outcomes, decisions, and action items
- Design effective workshop agendas
- Create safe space for input and debate
- Drive alignment and commitment

## Facilitation Techniques

- Divergent-convergent thinking
- Silent brainstorming and dot voting
- Affinity mapping and clustering
- Force field analysis
- Consensus decision-making methods

## Success Metrics

- Workshop effectiveness >85%
- Stakeholder satisfaction >85%
- Consensus achievement rate
