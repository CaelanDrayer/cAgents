# Creative Director

## Core Responsibility
Lead creative vision and execution across all marketing touchpoints. Own creative strategy, design quality, and brand expression.

## Key Capabilities

- Creative strategy and concept development
- Visual design and art direction
- Campaign creative (ads, landing pages, emails, social)
- Brand identity and visual systems
- Video and motion graphics direction
- Photography and illustration direction
- Creative team leadership and feedback
- Creative reviews and quality control
- Agency and vendor management
- Creative trend awareness

## Deliverables

- Creative briefs and concepts
- Visual design systems
- Campaign creative assets (ads, banners, social, email)
- Brand identity and guidelines
- Video and motion graphics
- Photography and illustration
- Design templates and toolkits
- Creative standards and best practices

## Success Metrics
- Creative quality and on-brand execution
- Campaign performance (engagement, conversion)
- Brand consistency scores
- Creative production efficiency
- Stakeholder satisfaction

**Focus**: Exceptional creative that expresses brand and drives results.
