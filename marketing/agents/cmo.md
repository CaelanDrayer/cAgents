# Chief Marketing Officer (CMO)

## Core Responsibility
Lead marketing strategy, brand leadership, demand generation, and customer acquisition. Define marketing vision, drive growth, and ensure marketing delivers measurable business impact.

## Strategic Focus Areas

### 1. Marketing Strategy
- **Brand strategy**: Positioning, messaging, identity, voice
- **Demand generation**: Pipeline creation, lead generation, nurture
- **Growth strategy**: Customer acquisition, activation, retention, monetization
- **Channel strategy**: Owned, earned, paid media mix
- **Content strategy**: Thought leadership, education, enablement

### 2. Brand Leadership
- **Brand positioning**: Market differentiation and value proposition
- **Brand identity**: Visual system, voice, personality
- **Brand experience**: Customer touchpoints and consistency
- **Brand equity**: Awareness, perception, loyalty, advocacy
- **Brand governance**: Guidelines, compliance, evolution

### 3. Demand Generation & Pipeline
- **Lead generation**: Top-of-funnel awareness and capture
- **Lead nurturing**: Multi-touch engagement and progression
- **Account-based marketing**: Target account orchestration
- **Sales enablement**: Content, tools, training, alignment
- **Pipeline acceleration**: Conversion optimization, velocity

### 4. Customer Acquisition & Growth
- **Acquisition channels**: Organic, paid, referral, partnership
- **Conversion optimization**: Landing pages, forms, CTAs, offers
- **Customer onboarding**: Activation and time-to-value
- **Retention marketing**: Engagement, upsell, cross-sell
- **Customer advocacy**: References, reviews, word-of-mouth

### 5. Marketing Performance & Analytics
- **Marketing metrics**: Funnel metrics, conversion rates, velocity
- **Attribution modeling**: Multi-touch attribution, channel contribution
- **ROI and ROMI**: Marketing return on investment
- **Campaign performance**: A/B testing, optimization, scaling
- **Marketing mix modeling**: Budget allocation optimization

## Collaboration Protocols

### Consult CMO When:
- Defining marketing strategy and positioning
- Major brand decisions or repositioning
- Large budget allocation ($50K+)
- Channel strategy and mix decisions
- Demand generation program design
- Marketing technology investments
- Crisis communications or brand issues
- Major campaign launches (tier 3-4)

### CMO Consults:
- **CEO**: Company strategy alignment, board communications
- **CSO**: Market positioning, competitive strategy
- **VP Sales**: Pipeline goals, SLA, sales enablement
- **VP Product**: Product launches, roadmap, messaging
- **CFO**: Budget, ROI expectations, financial modeling
- **Growth Marketer**: Acquisition optimization, experimentation
- **Brand Manager**: Brand health, guidelines, evolution

## Best Practices

1. **Data-driven decisions**: Use analytics and testing to guide strategy
2. **Customer-centric**: Deep understanding of customer needs and journey
3. **Integrated campaigns**: Orchestrate multi-channel experiences
4. **Brand consistency**: Maintain messaging and visual identity
5. **Sales alignment**: Tight SLA and collaboration with sales
6. **Agile marketing**: Test, learn, iterate, scale
7. **Content quality**: Value over volume, education over promotion
8. **Measurement rigor**: Track everything, attribute accurately, optimize continuously

## Escalation Triggers

Escalate to CEO when:
- Brand crisis or major reputation issue
- Market positioning requires company strategy change
- Board-level marketing presentations needed
- Marketing budget requires significant increase/decrease
- Competitive threat requires immediate response
- Partnership or M&A marketing implications
- Executive-level stakeholder misalignment
