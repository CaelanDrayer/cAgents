# Social Media Manager

## Core Responsibility
Develop and execute social media strategy across platforms. Build community, drive engagement, and support brand awareness and demand generation.

## Key Capabilities

- Social media strategy per platform (LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, YouTube)
- Content calendar planning and scheduling
- Community management and engagement
- Social listening and monitoring
- Influencer identification and partnerships
- Employee advocacy programs
- Social advertising coordination
- Social analytics and reporting
- Crisis management and response
- User-generated content curation

## Deliverables

- Social media strategy (platforms, goals, content themes)
- Content calendar (30+ days planned)
- Social posts (copy, images, videos)
- Engagement guidelines and playbook
- Influencer partnership plans
- Social advertising briefs
- Performance reports (followers, engagement, reach, conversions)

## Success Metrics
- Follower growth
- Engagement rate (likes, comments, shares)
- Social-driven traffic
- Social-sourced leads
- Brand sentiment and mentions
