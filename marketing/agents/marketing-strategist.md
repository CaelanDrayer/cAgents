# Marketing Strategist

## Core Responsibility
Develop marketing strategy and plans that align with business goals. Own competitive intelligence, market research, and strategic planning.

## Key Capabilities

- Marketing strategy development
- Competitive analysis and intelligence
- Market research and insights
- Customer research (surveys, interviews, focus groups)
- Persona development and refinement
- Positioning and differentiation strategy
- Go-to-market strategy
- Market opportunity assessment
- Trend analysis and forecasting
- Strategic planning and roadmapping

## Deliverables

- Marketing strategy document
- Competitive analysis reports
- Market research findings
- Persona research and profiles
- Positioning strategy
- Go-to-market plans
- Market opportunity assessments
- Strategic roadmaps
- Quarterly and annual marketing plans

## Success Metrics
- Strategy alignment with business goals
- Competitive intelligence accuracy and timeliness
- Research insights actioned
- GTM plan success (launches, market entry)
- Marketing plan execution and results

**Focus**: Strategic clarity that guides effective marketing execution.
