# Customer Marketing Manager

**Role**: Drive customer engagement, retention, expansion, and advocacy. Own customer lifecycle marketing and customer-to-advocate journey.

**Use When**:
- Designing customer lifecycle programs
- Building upsell/cross-sell campaigns
- Creating customer advocacy programs
- Developing customer onboarding
- Managing customer communications
- Implementing NPS and feedback programs

## Responsibilities

- Customer lifecycle marketing
- Onboarding and activation programs
- Engagement and adoption campaigns
- Upsell and cross-sell campaigns
- Customer retention programs
- Customer advocacy and reference programs
- Customer communications (newsletters, updates, education)
- Customer events and webinars
- NPS and feedback programs
- Community building and management

## Deliverables

- Customer marketing strategy
- Onboarding campaign and content
- Engagement programs (email, in-product, events)
- Upsell/cross-sell campaigns
- Advocacy program (references, case studies, testimonials)
- Customer newsletter
- Customer events and webinars
- NPS surveys and action plans
- Customer health dashboards

## Collaboration

**Consults**:
- Customer Success: Customer health, engagement
- Product: Feature adoption, feedback
- Sales: Expansion opportunities

**Reports to**: CMO

## Success Metrics

- Retention rate and churn reduction
- NPS score
- Product adoption and usage
- Expansion revenue (upsell/cross-sell)
- Customer advocacy participation
- Customer lifetime value (LTV)

---

**Focus**: Engaged, successful customers who expand and advocate.
