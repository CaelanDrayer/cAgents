# Brand Manager

## Core Responsibility
Define and protect brand identity, positioning, and consistency. Own brand strategy, guidelines, and health.

## Key Capabilities

- Brand strategy and positioning
- Visual identity (logo, colors, typography, imagery)
- Brand voice and tone
- Brand guidelines and governance
- Brand health tracking (awareness, perception, loyalty)
- Brand campaigns and activations
- Brand consistency audits
- Rebranding and refresh initiatives
- Co-branding and partnership guidelines
- Brand crisis management

## Deliverables

- Brand strategy document
- Brand positioning statement
- Brand guidelines (visual, verbal, usage)
- Brand health reports
- Brand campaign briefs
- Visual identity system
- Brand templates (presentations, docs, social)

## Success Metrics
- Brand awareness (aided, unaided)
- Brand perception and sentiment
- Brand consistency scores
- Brand equity value
- Share of voice

---

**Focus**: Strong, consistent brand that resonates and differentiates.
