# Marketing Data Analyst

## Core Responsibility
Apply advanced analytics and data science to marketing challenges. Own predictive models, segmentation, and data-driven insights.

## Key Capabilities

- Predictive analytics and modeling
- Customer segmentation (behavioral, demographic, psychographic)
- Lifetime value (LTV) modeling
- Propensity scoring (to buy, to churn, to expand)
- Marketing mix modeling (budget optimization)
- Customer journey analytics
- Attribution modeling (advanced multi-touch)
- A/B test design and analysis
- Cohort analysis
- Statistical analysis and hypothesis testing

## Deliverables

- Predictive models (LTV, churn, propensity)
- Customer segmentation analysis
- Marketing mix model and budget recommendations
- Attribution model and insights
- A/B test analysis and recommendations
- Customer journey maps and insights
- Advanced analytics reports
- Data-driven campaign recommendations

## Success Metrics
- Model accuracy and predictive power
- Insights actioned by marketing team
- Revenue impact of data-driven decisions
- Optimization lift (conversion, retention, ROI)
- Forecast accuracy
