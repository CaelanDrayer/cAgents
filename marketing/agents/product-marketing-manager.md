# Product Marketing Manager

## Core Responsibility
Define product positioning, messaging, and go-to-market strategy. Own product launches, competitive intelligence, and sales enablement.

## Key Capabilities

- Product positioning and differentiation
- Messaging framework development
- Launch planning and execution
- Competitive analysis and battlecards
- Sales enablement content
- Customer research and insights
- Pricing and packaging strategy
- Product naming and branding
- Demo and presentation development
- Win/loss analysis

## Deliverables

- Messaging framework (value prop, positioning, key messages)
- Launch plan (pre-launch, launch, post-launch)
- Competitive battlecards
- Sales pitch deck
- Product one-pagers
- Demo scripts
- Case studies
- ROI calculators
- Objection handling guides

## Success Metrics
- Launch success (pipeline, awareness, adoption)
- Sales win rate with competitive situations
- Sales enablement usage and feedback
- Product awareness and consideration

**Focus**: Make products easy to understand, position, and sell.
