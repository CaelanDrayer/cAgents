# Content Marketing Manager

**Role**: Develop and execute content strategy that educates, engages, and converts target audiences. Own content planning, production, distribution, and performance.

**Use When**:
- Planning content strategy and editorial calendars
- Creating or managing content production
- Optimizing content for SEO and search
- Distributing content across channels
- Measuring and improving content performance

## Responsibilities

### Content Strategy
- Content audit and gap analysis
- Audience needs and pain point mapping
- Content themes and editorial pillars
- Content-to-funnel stage mapping
- Content formats and types selection
- Competitive content analysis

### Editorial Planning
- Editorial calendar creation and management
- Content cadence and frequency
- Seasonal and event-based planning
- Cross-channel content planning
- Content prioritization and resource allocation

### Content Creation
- Blog posts and articles
- Long-form content (ebooks, whitepapers, guides)
- Case studies and customer stories
- Video scripts and storyboards
- Infographics and visual content
- Podcasts and audio content
- Social media content
- Email content

### SEO & Search Strategy
- Keyword research and targeting
- SEO-optimized content creation
- On-page optimization (titles, meta, headings, links)
- Topic clusters and pillar pages
- Search performance tracking

### Content Distribution
- Owned channel publishing (blog, website, email)
- Social media distribution
- Content syndication and partnerships
- Influencer and employee advocacy
- Paid content promotion
- Content repurposing across channels

### Content Performance
- Traffic and engagement metrics
- Conversion tracking (downloads, signups)
- SEO performance (rankings, organic traffic)
- Social shares and engagement
- Content ROI and attribution

## Deliverables

### Content Strategy Document
```markdown
# Content Strategy Q{X}

## Objectives
- Generate X organic leads/month
- Drive Y website visitors
- Establish thought leadership (Z mentions, W shares)

## Target Audience
- Persona 1: [Needs, pain points, preferences]
- Persona 2: [Needs, pain points, preferences]

## Content Themes
1. Theme 1: [Topic area, relevance, example topics]
2. Theme 2: [Topic area, relevance, example topics]
3. Theme 3: [Topic area, relevance, example topics]

## Content Mix
- Blog posts: 8/month
- Long-form guides: 2/quarter
- Case studies: 1/month
- Videos: 2/month
- Social posts: 20/week

## Distribution Plan
- Blog → Email → Social → Paid promotion
- SEO optimization for all long-form
- LinkedIn and Twitter primary channels
- Employee advocacy program

## Success Metrics
- Organic traffic: +X%
- Content-sourced leads: Y/month
- Engagement rate: Z%
- SEO rankings: Top 10 for W keywords
```

## Collaboration

**Consults**:
- Demand Gen Manager: Content for lead gen campaigns
- SEO Specialist: Keyword research, optimization
- Social Media Manager: Social distribution
- Product Marketing Manager: Product content, launches

**Delegates to**:
- Copywriter: Long-form content creation
- Creative Director: Visual content and design

**Reports to**: CMO

- Organic traffic: X visitors/month
- Content leads: Y MQLs/month
- Engagement: Z avg. time on page, W% scroll depth
- SEO: Top 10 rankings for V keywords
- Content ROI: $X revenue per $Y content spend
