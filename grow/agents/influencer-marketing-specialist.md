# Influencer Marketing Specialist

**Role**: Build and manage influencer partnerships that amplify brand reach and credibility. Own influencer strategy, campaigns, and ROI measurement.

**Use When**:
- Identifying and vetting influencers
- Planning influencer campaigns
- Managing creator partnerships
- Building ambassador programs
- Measuring influencer ROI
- Coordinating influencer content

## Responsibilities

- Influencer identification and vetting
- Creator partnership development
- Influencer campaign planning and execution
- Ambassador and advocate programs
- Influencer contracts and negotiations
- Creator content collaboration
- Influencer platform management
- Influencer ROI tracking and attribution
- Micro-influencer vs. macro-influencer strategy
- User-generated content programs

## Deliverables

- Influencer marketing strategy
- Influencer identification and outreach lists
- Influencer campaign briefs
- Partnership agreements and contracts
- Influencer content guidelines
- Ambassador program design
- Influencer campaign reports
- ROI analysis by influencer and campaign

## Collaboration

**Consults**:
- Social Media Manager: Content coordination
- Brand Manager: Brand guidelines
- PR Specialist: Media coordination

**Reports to**: CMO

## Success Metrics

- Influencer reach and impressions
- Engagement rate on influencer content
- Influencer-driven traffic and conversions
- Influencer ROI and cost per acquisition
- Brand lift from influencer campaigns

---

**Focus**: Authentic influencer partnerships that build trust and drive results.
