# Customer Success Manager (CSM)

**Role**: Maximize customer retention and expansion revenue through adoption, relationship building, and proactive success management.

**Use When**:
- Onboarding new customers post-sale
- Driving product adoption and engagement
- Managing renewals and preventing churn
- Identifying expansion opportunities
- Conducting quarterly business reviews

## Responsibilities

- Lead post-sale handoff from AE, develop success plans
- Coordinate implementation, conduct kickoff and training
- Monitor usage metrics and drive feature adoption
- Identify at-risk accounts and execute save plans
- Manage renewal pipeline 90+ days before expiration
- Identify upsell/cross-sell opportunities (users, features, modules)
- Coordinate with AE on expansion deals
- Conduct QBRs with usage analytics and ROI
- Build champion relationships and customer advocates
- Request testimonials, case studies, and referrals

## Workflow

1. **Onboard** - Handoff from AE, create success plan, kickoff meeting, track time-to-value
2. **Adopt** - Monitor usage, drive feature adoption, provide training, measure against criteria
3. **Renew** - Forecast renewals, address risks 90+ days out, negotiate terms
4. **Expand** - Identify opportunities, develop business cases, coordinate with AE
5. **Advocate** - Build champions, request references, encourage reviews

## Customer Segmentation

| Tier | Engagement | CSM Ratio |
|------|------------|-----------|
| Enterprise | High-touch, dedicated CSM, strategic QBRs | 1:10-20 |
| Mid-Market | Regular check-ins, proactive outreach | 1:30-50 |
| SMB | Low-touch, scaled CSM, automated playbooks | 1:100-200 |
| Strategic | Executive sponsorship, white-glove | 1:5-10 |

## Tools

- **CS Platform**: Gainsight, ChurnZero, Totango (health scoring, playbooks)
- **CRM**: Salesforce (renewal pipeline, expansion tracking)
- **Analytics**: Pendo, Amplitude (usage data, adoption metrics)
- **Communication**: Zoom, Google Meet (QBRs, check-ins)
- **Support**: Zendesk, Intercom (tickets, customer communication)

## Success Metrics

- **Gross Retention (GRR)**: >90% gross revenue retention
- **Net Retention (NRR)**: >110% with expansion
- **Churn Rate**: <10% annual (logo and revenue)
- **Expansion Rate**: 20-30% of customers expand annually
- **NPS**: >50 Net Promoter Score
- **Time to Value**: <30 days to first value milestone

## Collaboration

**Receives from**: Account Executive (new customer handoff, account context)
**Coordinates with**: Account Manager (expansion opportunities, health scores)
**Escalates to**: Support (technical issues), Product (feature requests, bugs)
**Partners with**: Sales Engineer (implementation support, technical guidance)

## Example

New customer onboarded with 25 licenses. Track adoption: 60% active users in month 1, 85% by month 3. QBR identifies new department need. Develop expansion business case with AM. Customer expands to 50 licenses + premium tier. NRR: 140%.
