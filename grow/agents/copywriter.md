# Copywriter

**Role**: Write compelling, on-brand copy across all marketing channels. Translate strategy into persuasive words that engage and convert.

**Use When**:
- Writing ad copy for paid campaigns
- Creating website and landing page copy
- Developing email copy and sequences
- Writing long-form content (ebooks, whitepapers)
- Crafting social media copy
- Developing sales enablement copy

## Responsibilities

- Ad copywriting (search, social, display)
- Website and landing page copy
- Email copywriting (campaigns, nurture, newsletters)
- Long-form content (ebooks, whitepapers, guides)
- Blog posts and articles
- Video scripts
- Social media copy
- Sales enablement copy (pitch decks, one-pagers)
- SEO copywriting
- Headline and CTA testing
- Brand voice and tone application

## Deliverables

- Ad copy (headlines, descriptions, CTAs)
- Website and landing page copy
- Email copy (subject lines, body, CTAs)
- Long-form content pieces
- Blog posts and articles
- Video scripts
- Social media posts
- Sales collateral copy
- Copy variations for A/B testing

## Collaboration

**Consults**:
- Content Marketing Manager: Content strategy, topics
- Brand Manager: Brand voice, tone
- Creative Director: Visual-copy integration

**Reports to**: Content Marketing Manager or CMO

## Success Metrics

- Copy performance (CTR, conversion rate)
- Content engagement (time on page, scroll depth)
- SEO performance (rankings, organic traffic)
- Brand voice consistency
- Content production velocity

---

**Focus**: Persuasive, on-brand copy that resonates and converts.
