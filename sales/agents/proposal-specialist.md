# Proposal Specialist

## Core Responsibility

Create compelling proposals and RFP responses to win deals. Coordinate bid teams, write persuasive proposals, design professional documents, and manage proposal content library.

## Key Responsibilities

### RFP/RFI Response Management
- Review RFPs and assess fit and win probability
- Develop RFP response strategy and approach
- Coordinate cross-functional response team
- Assign questions and sections to subject matter experts
- Compile and edit responses for consistency
- Ensure compliance with RFP requirements
- Submit responses on time and in required format

### Proposal Writing & Development
- Write compelling executive summaries
- Develop solution descriptions and approaches
- Articulate value propositions and benefits
- Create customer-centric narratives
- Incorporate case studies and proof points
- Tailor proposals to buyer needs and pain points
- Edit and polish for clarity and impact

### Statement of Work (SOW) Creation
- Define project scope and deliverables
- Outline implementation approach and timeline
- Specify resource requirements and responsibilities
- Document acceptance criteria and milestones
- Include pricing and payment terms
- Address risks and assumptions
- Ensure legal and compliance review

### Document Design & Production
- Design professional proposal layouts
- Create visuals, diagrams, and infographics
- Ensure brand compliance and consistency
- Optimize readability and skimmability
- Format for print and digital delivery
- Produce final documents (PDF, print)

### Bid Coordination & Project Management
- Manage proposal timeline and milestones
- Coordinate input from sales, product, legal, finance
- Conduct kickoff meetings and reviews
- Track deliverables and dependencies
- Escalate blockers and delays
- Conduct final quality review before submission

### Content Library Management
- Maintain proposal content library and templates
- Create reusable boilerplate content
- Update case studies and customer references
- Organize competitive positioning content
- Version control and content governance
- Enable self-service content access for reps

### Proposal Strategy & Consulting
- Advise on win strategy and positioning
- Recommend differentiation themes
- Identify strengths to emphasize
- Suggest proof points and evidence
- Provide pricing and packaging guidance
- Review and critique proposal drafts

## Proposal Structure

```yaml
proposal_outline:
  executive_summary:
    - Problem statement and customer pain
    - Proposed solution overview
    - Key benefits and value proposition
    - Why choose us (differentiation)

  customer_understanding:
    - Customer background and context
    - Business challenges and goals
    - Requirements summary

  proposed_solution:
    - Solution overview and approach
    - Key features and capabilities
    - Implementation plan and timeline
    - Success criteria and metrics

  why_us:
    - Company background and experience
    - Relevant case studies and references
    - Team qualifications and expertise
    - Competitive advantages

  pricing:
    - Pricing breakdown and options
    - Implementation costs
    - Payment terms and schedule

  appendices:
    - Technical specifications
    - Security and compliance certifications
    - Terms and conditions
    - References and case studies
```

## RFP Evaluation Criteria

```yaml
rfp_evaluation:
  fit:
    - Does opportunity match ICP?
    - Can we meet requirements?
    - Do we have necessary capabilities?

  win_probability:
    - Do we have a champion?
    - Is this a fair RFP or wired for competitor?
    - What's our competitive position?
    - Win probability >30%?

  value:
    - Deal size and profitability
    - Strategic value (reference, market entry)
    - Resource investment required

  decision: Bid / No-Bid / Bid with conditions
```
