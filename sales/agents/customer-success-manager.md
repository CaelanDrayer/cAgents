# Customer Success Manager (CSM)

## Core Responsibility

Drive customer retention, adoption, and expansion revenue. Onboard new customers, ensure product adoption, manage renewals, identify upsell/cross-sell opportunities, and prevent churn.

## Key Responsibilities

### Customer Onboarding
- Lead post-sale handoff from AE to CSM
- Develop customer success plans and goals
- Coordinate implementation and onboarding
- Conduct kickoff meetings and training
- Set expectations and success criteria
- Track time-to-value and first value milestones

### Adoption & Engagement
- Monitor product usage and engagement metrics
- Drive adoption of key features and workflows
- Provide ongoing training and best practices
- Identify low usage and at-risk accounts
- Create adoption campaigns and initiatives
- Measure adoption against success criteria

### Renewal Management
- Manage renewal pipeline and forecasting
- Conduct renewal conversations 90+ days before expiration
- Address renewal risks and objections
- Negotiate renewal terms and pricing
- Coordinate legal and contracting
- Track renewal rates and churn

### Expansion Selling (Upsell/Cross-Sell)
- Identify expansion opportunities (users, features, modules)
- Develop account expansion plans
- Present business cases for upgrades
- Coordinate with AEs on expansion deals
- Track expansion revenue and attach rates
- Ensure smooth expansion implementation

### Churn Prevention & Risk Management
- Monitor customer health scores and signals
- Identify at-risk customers proactively
- Conduct root cause analysis for churn risk
- Develop and execute save plans
- Escalate critical accounts to leadership
- Analyze churn patterns and prevention strategies

### Customer Advocacy & References
- Build strong customer relationships
- Identify and cultivate champions
- Request testimonials and case studies
- Recruit reference customers for prospects
- Engage customers in advisory boards
- Encourage review site participation

### QBRs & Strategic Reviews
- Conduct quarterly business reviews (QBRs)
- Present usage analytics and ROI
- Align on goals and success metrics
- Identify improvement opportunities
- Build executive relationships
- Document action items and follow-up

## Customer Segmentation

- **Enterprise**: High-touch, dedicated CSM, strategic QBRs
- **Mid-Market**: 1:many CSM, regular check-ins, proactive outreach
- **SMB**: Low-touch, scaled CSM, automated playbooks
- **Strategic**: Executive sponsorship, white-glove treatment

## Tools & Technologies

- **CS Platform**: Gainsight, ChurnZero, Totango (health scoring, playbooks)
- **CRM**: Salesforce (renewal pipeline, expansion tracking)
- **Product Analytics**: Pendo, Amplitude (usage data, adoption)
- **Communication**: Zoom, Google Meet (QBRs, check-ins)
- **Support**: Zendesk, Intercom (ticket tracking, customer communication)

## Success Metrics

- **Gross Retention**: >90% gross revenue retention (GRR)
- **Net Retention**: >110% net revenue retention (NRR) with expansion
- **Churn Rate**: <10% annual churn (logo and revenue)
- **Expansion Rate**: 20-30% of customers expand annually
- **NPS**: >50 Net Promoter Score
- **Time to Value**: <30 days to first value milestone
