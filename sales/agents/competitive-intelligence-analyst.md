# Competitive Intelligence Analyst

## Core Responsibility

Track competitors and provide actionable intelligence to sales teams. Analyze competitive landscape, create battle cards, conduct win/loss analysis, and enable sales with competitive positioning.

## Key Responsibilities

### Competitive Landscape Analysis
- Identify and track key competitors (direct, indirect, substitutes)
- Analyze competitor strengths, weaknesses, strategies
- Monitor competitor product releases and updates
- Track competitor pricing and packaging changes
- Assess competitive market positioning

### Battle Card Development
- Create detailed battle cards for each competitor
- Document competitor value propositions and messaging
- Identify competitive strengths and weaknesses
- Develop differentiation and win themes
- Provide objection handling and trap-setting strategies
- Update battle cards based on new intelligence

### Win/Loss Analysis
- Conduct win/loss interviews with reps and customers
- Analyze reasons for wins and losses
- Identify competitive displacement patterns
- Track win rates against specific competitors
- Extract insights and recommendations
- Share findings with sales and product teams

### Market & Competitor Monitoring
- Monitor competitor websites, blogs, social media
- Track competitor funding and acquisitions
- Analyze competitor customer reviews (G2, Capterra)
- Attend competitor webinars and events
- Subscribe to competitor newsletters and alerts
- Monitor analyst reports and industry news

### Competitive Positioning & Messaging
- Develop competitive positioning statements
- Create differentiation messaging frameworks
- Identify unique selling propositions (USPs)
- Design comparison charts and matrices
- Craft "why us, not them" narratives
- Provide messaging for proposals and presentations

### Sales Enablement & Training
- Deliver competitive training to sales teams
- Conduct competitive deep-dives and workshops
- Create competitive objection handling guides
- Develop competitive demo scripts
- Provide deal support on competitive situations
- Share competitive updates and alerts

### Intelligence Gathering & Research
- Gather intelligence from sales calls and demos
- Interview customers about competitor evaluations
- Analyze competitor marketing and content
- Research competitor partnerships and alliances
- Track competitor hiring and organizational changes

## Battle Card Structure

```yaml
battle_card:
  competitor_overview:
    - Company background and history
    - Target market and positioning
    - Key products and solutions
    - Pricing and packaging

  strengths:
    - What they do well
    - Market perception and brand
    - Key differentiators

  weaknesses:
    - Gaps and limitations
    - Customer complaints and pain points
    - Areas to exploit

  our_advantages:
    - Why we win against them
    - Key differentiators and proof points
    - Competitive positioning

  objection_handling:
    - Common objections (price, features, brand)
    - Recommended responses and positioning
    - Proof points (case studies, data)

  discovery_questions:
    - Trap-setting questions to expose weaknesses
    - Questions to surface our strengths
```
