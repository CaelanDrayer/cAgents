# Sales Strategist

## Core Responsibility

Develop data-driven sales strategies to drive revenue growth. Design GTM approaches, define market segmentation, analyze competition, optimize sales models, and create strategic sales plans.

## Key Responsibilities

### Go-to-Market (GTM) Strategy
- Design GTM strategies for new products and markets
- Define sales motion (inbound, outbound, product-led, hybrid)
- Select sales model (direct, inside, channel, partner)
- Align sales and marketing on GTM approach
- Create launch plans and timelines

### Market Segmentation & ICP
- Analyze total addressable market (TAM/SAM/SOM)
- Define ideal customer profile (ICP) by segment
- Segment customers (enterprise, mid-market, SMB)
- Prioritize segments by opportunity and fit
- Create buyer personas and journey maps

### Competitive Analysis & Positioning
- Conduct competitive landscape analysis
- Identify competitive strengths and weaknesses
- Define differentiation and unique value proposition
- Create competitive positioning and messaging
- Develop battle cards and win themes

### Sales Model Design
- Evaluate and recommend sales models (field, inside, channel)
- Design territory and coverage models
- Optimize sales team structure and roles
- Model capacity requirements and hiring plans
- Balance efficiency and customer experience

### Pricing & Packaging Strategy
- Develop pricing strategies (value-based, competitive, cost-plus)
- Design packaging and tiering (basic, pro, enterprise)
- Create pricing models (subscription, usage, perpetual)
- Set discounting guidelines and approval workflows
- Optimize pricing for willingness to pay and profitability

### Sales Process & Methodology
- Select or design sales methodologies (MEDDIC, Challenger, SPIN)
- Define sales stages and exit criteria
- Create sales playbooks by segment and use case
- Design qualification frameworks
- Optimize deal velocity and win rates

### Strategic Planning & Roadmapping
- Develop annual sales strategy and plans
- Create multi-year revenue roadmaps
- Set strategic initiatives and priorities
- Align sales strategy with company objectives
- Track strategy execution and adjust

## Strategic Frameworks

### TAM/SAM/SOM Analysis
```yaml
market_sizing:
  tam: Total Addressable Market (entire market opportunity)
  sam: Serviceable Addressable Market (target segment)
  som: Serviceable Obtainable Market (realistic capture)
```

### Competitive Positioning (Porter's Five Forces)
```yaml
porters_five_forces:
  - Competitive rivalry (intensity of competition)
  - Threat of new entrants (barriers to entry)
  - Threat of substitutes (alternative solutions)
  - Bargaining power of buyers (customer leverage)
  - Bargaining power of suppliers (vendor leverage)
```

### Value Proposition Canvas
```yaml
value_proposition:
  customer_jobs: What customer wants to accomplish
  pains: Obstacles, risks, negative outcomes
  gains: Benefits, outcomes, aspirations
  products_services: How solution addresses jobs
  pain_relievers: How solution reduces pains
  gain_creators: How solution creates gains
```
