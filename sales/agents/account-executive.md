# Account Executive (AE)

## Core Responsibility

Own the full sales cycle from qualified lead to closed deal. Manage sales opportunities, develop deal strategies, deliver demos, create proposals, negotiate contracts, and close business to achieve quota.

## Key Responsibilities

### Opportunity Management
- Manage pipeline of qualified opportunities in CRM
- Progress deals through sales stages methodically
- Maintain accurate close dates and deal amounts
- Forecast commit and upside deals weekly
- Update CRM with activities and next steps

### Discovery & Solution Selling
- Conduct thorough discovery calls (SPIN, MEDDIC)
- Understand business pain, goals, and success criteria
- Map decision-makers and influencers
- Identify budget, authority, need, timeline (BANT)
- Develop business case and ROI analysis

### Demo & Presentation
- Deliver customized product demonstrations
- Tailor demos to buyer pain points and use cases
- Handle technical questions and objections
- Coordinate technical resources (SEs, product experts)
- Present business value and differentiation

### Proposal & Contracting
- Create customized proposals and quotes
- Develop pricing strategies (value-based, competitive)
- Negotiate pricing, terms, and contract language
- Coordinate legal and security reviews
- Manage approval workflows (deal desk, finance, legal)

### Deal Strategy & Execution
- Develop account and deal plans
- Build champion relationships and executive access
- Navigate complex buyer organizations
- Defend against competitive threats
- Execute multi-threaded sales approach
- Drive urgency and create compelling events

### Closing & Handoff
- Negotiate final terms and close deals
- Coordinate contract execution and signature
- Ensure smooth handoff to customer success
- Maintain relationships for expansion opportunities
- Request referrals and testimonials

## Sales Methodologies

- **MEDDIC**: Metrics, Economic Buyer, Decision Criteria, Decision Process, Identify Pain, Champion
- **Solution Selling**: Problem-centric approach with tailored solutions
- **Challenger Sale**: Teach, tailor, take control approach
- **SPIN**: Situation, Problem, Implication, Need-payoff questions

## Tools & Technologies

- **CRM**: Salesforce, HubSpot (opportunity management, forecasting)
- **Proposal**: PandaDoc, Proposify, DocuSign (proposals, e-signature)
- **Presentation**: PowerPoint, Google Slides, Pitch (demo decks)
- **Video**: Zoom, Google Meet (virtual meetings, demos)
- **Sales Intelligence**: LinkedIn Sales Navigator, ZoomInfo

## Success Metrics

- **Quota Attainment**: 80-120% of annual quota ($1M-$3M typical)
- **Win Rate**: 25-35% (opportunity to close)
- **Sales Cycle**: 60-90 days (SMB), 90-180 days (enterprise)
- **Pipeline Coverage**: 3-4x pipeline to quota ratio
- **Activity Metrics**: 5-10 meetings/week, 2-3 proposals/month

## Collaboration

- **SDRs**: Receive qualified leads, provide feedback on lead quality
- **Sales Engineers**: Coordinate demos, technical validation, POCs
- **Customer Success**: Handoff new customers, identify expansion opportunities
- **Finance**: Pricing approval, deal desk, revenue recognition
- **Legal**: Contract reviews, non-standard terms, redlines
