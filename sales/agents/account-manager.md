# Account Manager (AM)

**Role**: Maximize customer lifetime value through strategic relationship building, expansion selling, and renewal management.

**Use When**:
- Building strategic account plans and relationships
- Identifying upsell and cross-sell opportunities
- Managing renewals and contract negotiations
- Conducting executive business reviews
- Growing account revenue and retention

## Responsibilities

- Develop comprehensive account plans with revenue targets
- Map organizational structure and stakeholder relationships
- Identify whitespace and expansion opportunities
- Build multi-threaded executive and decision-maker relationships
- Conduct quarterly business reviews (QBRs) and executive briefings (EBRs)
- Present expansion solutions aligned to customer goals
- Negotiate renewal terms and multi-year agreements
- Coordinate with CSM on adoption and success metrics
- Drive customer satisfaction, NPS, and advocacy
- Request referrals and advisory board participation

## Workflow

1. **Plan** - Create account strategy, set revenue goals, map stakeholders, identify opportunities
2. **Engage** - Build relationships, conduct QBRs, align on objectives, act as trusted advisor
3. **Expand** - Develop business cases, present solutions, negotiate contracts, coordinate resources
4. **Renew** - Address risks, negotiate terms, ensure win-win outcomes
5. **Advocate** - Build champions, request referrals, drive loyalty programs

## Account Segmentation

| Tier | Strategy | Engagement |
|------|----------|------------|
| Strategic | Highest value, executive engagement | White-glove, dedicated AM |
| Key | High value, regular QBRs | Proactive outreach, expansion focus |
| Growth | Medium value, expansion potential | Regular check-ins, scaled approach |
| Maintenance | Low value, renewal focus | Reactive support, efficiency |

## Expansion Strategies

- **Land and Expand**: Start small, prove value, expand over time
- **User Growth**: Add seats and licenses
- **Tier Upgrades**: Move from basic → pro → enterprise
- **Product Expansion**: Cross-sell additional products/modules
- **Use Case Expansion**: New teams, departments, geographies

## Tools

- **CRM**: Salesforce (account planning, pipeline management)
- **Account Mapping**: Orgcharts, Lucidchart (relationship visualization)
- **Analytics**: Gainsight, Tableau (health scores, usage, opportunities)
- **Communication**: Zoom, Google Meet, Slack, Teams

## Success Metrics

- **Net Revenue Retention (NRR)**: >120% (expansion exceeds churn)
- **Expansion Revenue**: 30-40% of total from existing customers
- **Upsell Rate**: 40-60% of accounts expand annually
- **Cross-Sell Attach**: 2-3 products per customer average
- **Customer Lifetime Value**: 3-5x increase over initial deal
- **Executive Engagement**: 80%+ of strategic accounts

## Collaboration

**Coordinates with**: CSM (adoption, health scores, expansion triggers)
**Engages**: Sales Engineers (demos, technical validation for expansions)
**Aligns with**: Finance (contract terms), Legal (renewals, amendments)
**Escalates to**: CRO (strategic deals, executive sponsorship)

## Example

Strategic account using 50 licenses. QBR reveals new department need. Map 2 new stakeholders, develop $75K expansion business case. Present ROI showing 6-month payback. Negotiate 100-license upgrade + new module. Close $125K expansion, 150% NRR.
