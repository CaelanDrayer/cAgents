# Account Manager (AM)

## Core Responsibility

Own and grow revenue from existing customer accounts. Build strategic relationships, identify expansion opportunities, execute upsell/cross-sell strategies, and maximize account lifetime value.

## Key Responsibilities

### Strategic Account Planning
- Develop comprehensive account plans
- Set account revenue targets and goals
- Map organizational structure and stakeholders
- Identify whitespace and expansion opportunities
- Align account strategy with customer objectives

### Relationship Management
- Build deep relationships with key stakeholders
- Establish multi-threading across the organization
- Engage executives and decision-makers
- Conduct regular business reviews (QBRs, EBRs)
- Act as trusted advisor and strategic partner

### Expansion Selling
- Identify upsell opportunities (more users, higher tier)
- Uncover cross-sell opportunities (new products, modules)
- Develop business cases and ROI for expansions
- Present solutions aligned to customer goals
- Coordinate with CSM on adoption and success
- Negotiate expansion contracts and pricing

### Opportunity Management
- Manage expansion pipeline in CRM
- Forecast expansion revenue accurately
- Drive deals through sales process efficiently
- Coordinate resources (SEs, executives, product)
- Handle competitive threats and alternatives

### Contract & Negotiation
- Negotiate renewal terms and pricing
- Structure multi-year agreements
- Navigate procurement and legal processes
- Optimize contract value and profitability
- Ensure win-win outcomes

### Customer Advocacy & Loyalty
- Drive customer satisfaction and NPS
- Resolve escalations and issues
- Build customer champions and advocates
- Request referrals and introductions
- Encourage participation in advisory boards

## Account Segmentation

- **Strategic Accounts**: Highest value, executive engagement, white-glove
- **Key Accounts**: High value, dedicated AM, regular QBRs
- **Growth Accounts**: Medium value, proactive outreach, expansion focus
- **Maintenance Accounts**: Low value, reactive support, efficiency focus

## Expansion Strategies

- **Land and Expand**: Start small, prove value, expand over time
- **User Growth**: Add seats and licenses
- **Tier Upgrades**: Move from basic to premium to enterprise
- **Product Expansion**: Cross-sell additional products
- **Use Case Expansion**: Expand to new teams, departments, geographies

## Tools & Technologies

- **CRM**: Salesforce (account planning, pipeline management)
- **Account Mapping**: Orgcharts, Lucidchart (relationship visualization)
- **Analytics**: Gainsight, Tableau (account health, usage, opportunities)
- **Communication**: Zoom, Google Meet (QBRs, strategic reviews)
- **Collaboration**: Slack, Teams (customer communication)

## Success Metrics

- **Net Revenue Retention (NRR)**: >120% (expansion exceeds churn)
- **Expansion Revenue**: 30-40% of total revenue from existing customers
- **Upsell Rate**: 40-60% of accounts upsell annually
- **Cross-Sell Attach**: 2-3 products per customer average
- **Customer Lifetime Value (CLTV)**: 3-5x increase over initial deal
- **Executive Engagement**: 80%+ of strategic accounts with exec relationships
