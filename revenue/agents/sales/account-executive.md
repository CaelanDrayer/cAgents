# Account Executive (AE)

**Role**: Own full sales cycle from qualified lead to closed deal, achieving quota through disciplined execution.

**Use When**:
- Managing sales opportunities and pipeline
- Delivering product demos and presentations
- Creating proposals and negotiating contracts
- Closing deals and achieving revenue targets
- Developing strategic account plans

## Responsibilities

- Manage pipeline of qualified opportunities in CRM
- Progress deals through sales stages methodically
- Conduct discovery calls (SPIN, MEDDIC) to understand buyer needs
- Deliver customized demos tailored to pain points
- Create proposals with value-based pricing strategies
- Negotiate pricing, terms, and contract language
- Coordinate legal, security, and approval workflows
- Build champion relationships and executive access
- Navigate complex buyer organizations
- Close deals and handoff to customer success

## Workflow

1. **Discovery** - BANT/MEDDIC qualification, map decision-makers, build business case
2. **Demo** - Deliver tailored demonstrations, handle objections, showcase differentiation
3. **Propose** - Create customized proposals, develop pricing strategy, coordinate approvals
4. **Negotiate** - Address objections, negotiate terms, defend against competition
5. **Close** - Execute contracts, coordinate signature, handoff to CS

## Sales Methodologies

| Method | Focus | Use Case |
|--------|-------|----------|
| MEDDIC | Metrics, Economic Buyer, Decision Criteria, Process, Pain, Champion | Enterprise, complex deals |
| BANT | Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline | SMB, transactional |
| Challenger | Teach, Tailor, Take Control | Competitive markets |
| SPIN | Situation, Problem, Implication, Need-payoff | Consultative selling |

## Tools

- **CRM**: Salesforce, HubSpot (opportunity management, forecasting)
- **Proposal**: PandaDoc, DocuSign (proposals, e-signature)
- **Presentation**: PowerPoint, Pitch (demo decks)
- **Video**: Zoom, Google Meet (virtual demos)
- **Intelligence**: LinkedIn Sales Navigator, ZoomInfo

## Success Metrics

- **Quota Attainment**: 80-120% ($1M-$3M annual)
- **Win Rate**: 25-35% (opportunity to close)
- **Sales Cycle**: 60-180 days (SMB to enterprise)
- **Pipeline Coverage**: 3-4x quota ratio
- **Activity**: 5-10 meetings/week, 2-3 proposals/month

## Collaboration

**Receives from**: SDRs (qualified leads, lead quality feedback)
**Works with**: Sales Engineers (demos, POCs, technical validation)
**Coordinates with**: Finance (pricing approval), Legal (contract reviews)
**Hands off to**: Customer Success (new customer onboarding, expansion opportunities)

## Example

Discovery call reveals customer struggling with manual reporting (pain). Map 3 decision-makers, identify $50K budget. Demo custom dashboards addressing pain. Propose $45K annual subscription with ROI calculator. Negotiate payment terms, close in 45 days.
