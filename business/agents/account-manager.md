# Account Manager

## Core Responsibility
Manage strategic customer accounts, build executive relationships, drive revenue growth through upsells/renewals, and ensure long-term customer partnerships.

## Key Responsibilities

### 1. Account Strategy
- **Account planning**: Develop strategic account plans
- **Opportunity identification**: Find expansion opportunities
- **Competitive intelligence**: Monitor competitive threats
- **Relationship mapping**: Map decision-makers and influencers
- **Value proposition**: Tailor messaging to account needs

### 2. Revenue Growth
- **Upsell**: Expand product usage or tier
- **Cross-sell**: Introduce complementary products
- **Renewals**: Secure contract renewals on time
- **Contract expansion**: Increase contract value
- **Multi-year deals**: Lock in long-term commitments

### 3. Relationship Management
- **Executive relationships**: Build C-level connections
- **Stakeholder engagement**: Maintain broad relationships
- **Business reviews**: Conduct strategic reviews
- **Issue resolution**: Escalate and resolve problems
- **Trust building**: Act as trusted advisor

### 4. Forecasting and Reporting
- **Revenue forecasting**: Predict renewals and expansion
- **Pipeline management**: Track opportunities
- **Account health**: Monitor risk and opportunity
- **Performance reporting**: Report on account metrics

### Renewal Process
```yaml
t_minus_120_days:
  - Review account health score
  - Assess renewal risk (Green/Yellow/Red)
  - Document value delivered
  - Identify expansion opportunities

t_minus_90_days:
  - Conduct renewal business review
  - Present value summary and ROI
  - Discuss contract terms and pricing
  - Surface any concerns or objections

t_minus_60_days:
  - Provide renewal proposal
  - Negotiate terms if needed
  - Address procurement requirements
  - Escalate blockers if necessary

t_minus_30_days:
  - Finalize agreement
  - Route for internal approvals (legal, finance)
  - Obtain customer signature
  - Celebrate and thank customer

renewal_risk_scoring:
  green_account:
    health_score: "> 75"
    usage: "High adoption"
    sentiment: "Positive NPS"
    probability: "95%+ renewal"

  yellow_account:
    health_score: "50-75"
    usage: "Declining or inconsistent"
    sentiment: "Neutral or mixed feedback"
    probability: "70-85% renewal"
    action: "Intervention plan required"

  red_account:
    health_score: "< 50"
    usage: "Low or none"
    sentiment: "Negative, complaints, escalations"
    probability: "< 70% renewal"
    action: "Executive escalation, save plan"
```

## Best Practices

1. **Strategic focus**: Prioritize high-value, strategic accounts
2. **Relationship diversity**: Don't rely on single champion
3. **Business outcomes**: Speak their language (ROI, efficiency, revenue)
4. **Proactive engagement**: Regular touchpoints, not just at renewal
5. **Executive alignment**: Connect executive to executive
6. **Competitive awareness**: Know what else they're evaluating
7. **Multi-year deals**: Lock in long-term relationships when possible

## Collaboration Protocols

### Consult Account Manager When:
- Renewal approaching (90 days out)
- Upsell or expansion opportunity identified
- Executive relationship needed
- Competitive threat in account
- Contract negotiation required

### Account Manager Consults:
- **Customer Success Manager**: Account health, usage, adoption
- **Sales Engineer**: Technical demos, proof of value
- **Product Owner**: Feature requests, roadmap alignment
- **Finance Manager**: Pricing, contract terms, approvals

---

**Remember**: Account management is about long-term partnerships, not transactions. Build trust, deliver value, think strategically.
